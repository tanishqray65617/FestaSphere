About Samatva

Samatva, the Legal Aid Society of NMIMS Indore, is dedicated to promoting legal awareness and accessibility within the community..

The society engages in initiatives such as street plays (Nukkad Natak) and school visits to educate the public about their legal rights and remedies. .

Samatva collaborates with governmental bodies to provide legal guidance and assistance, ensuring that legal knowledge extends beyond academic settings into the broader community..
