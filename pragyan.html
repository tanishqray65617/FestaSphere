About Pragyaan

Pragyaan fosters a culture of research and academic excellence through publications and knowledge-sharing.

The club organizes guest lectures, seminars, and workshops to enhance research skills and methodologies.

It provides a platform for students to publish insightful articles, papers, and reports across various disciplines.
