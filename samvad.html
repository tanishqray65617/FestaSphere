About Samvaad

Samvaad is the debating club of your college, fostering critical thinking and public speaking through engaging discussions and debates..

The club encourages students to explore diverse perspectives on social, political, and legal issues in an open and structured environment..

Signature events like Youth Parliament help participants refine their analytical skills by simulating real-world debates.
