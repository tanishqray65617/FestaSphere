About Sangam

Sangam is a cultural club that celebrates traditions and festivals through a variety of events. The club organizes performances, music, and dance, giving students the chance to connect with their cultural heritage and showcase their talents.

The highlight of Sangam’s events is Nmotsav, an eagerly awaited cultural fest. It features dance, music, and other performances, bringing students together to celebrate creativity and cultural pride.

Along with Nmotsav, Sangam hosts various dance and music competitions, encouraging students to express themselves artistically. The club fosters unity, tradition, and the joy of cultural celebration throughout the year.
